About Us

It has been nearly 8 years since we started this restaurant, and we have successfully opened branches in different parts of the country, namely Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Johor Bahru and Ipoh.

The reason why our restaurant has survived so long is because we serve every customer with our hearts wherever they are. We are not sloppy when it comes to ingredients, we personally select the best ingredients and prepare each dish with care every day. In this way, clients can appreciate our dedication.

Not only that, our restaurant redefines what food should be like, we are committed to making food that will impress our customers, and today we are successfully incorporating new elements into many traditional recipes. And won great appreciation from customers.
